My Top Three Games of All Time

The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time

This one should come as no surprise to those of you that know video games. This is widely regarded as one of the best games of all time, and for good reason. From start to finish Ocarina of Time captivates you with a powerful story, interesting new locations, and one small boys inspirational quest to save the world. I had no trouble choosing this as one of my top three and will continue to replay it for years to come.

Halo: Combat Evolved

This game was revolutionary in its time and changed the first person shooter genre forever. Utitilizing the offset-stick controller of the original xbox, it brought a new aspect of control that had rarely been seen before. This game is fundamental to my love of video games, and will always hold a place in my top 3. The story was mindblowing to me, and the level design was way ahead of its time. I can not count the amount of memories I have playing this exceptional game.

Star Wars: Knights of The Old Republic

Star Wars Knights of The Old Republic is the first entry in a widely loved game series. The rich story telling that takes place in this game, is something that comes once a decade in video games. Bioware is one of my favourite developers and this may just be their masterpiece. As you work towards becoming a Jedi, you gain exciting new powers and encounter some shocking twists along the way. The game has become a cult classic, and has earned its place in my top three.
