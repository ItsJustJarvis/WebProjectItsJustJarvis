About Me

I've always dreamed of being a developer...

My whole childhood, I spent my time exploring interesting new worlds through video games. I grew a passion for technology the second my hands touched the controller. I am excited to start my career in tech to take that passion further, and bring exciting new ideas into our world.

Outside of tech, I am passionate about music. I grew up listening to albums with my dad, and developed a taste for classic rock and roll. Led Zeppelin, Pink Floyd, Supertramp... these are the greats. I have begun to learn guitar myself, and have amassed a rather large record collection.

My Partners In Life...

Kayla is my best friend and life partner. She is one of the strongest women I have ever known, and has been such an essential part of my growth as a person. We have had many adventures over the years, and we've lived in some beautiful places. I can't wait to see what else life has in store for us.

Archie is my newest sidekick. He is a Korean Jindo mix breed, and though he may be small he is stronger than he looks. Dont get between him and his treats, or you may have a scrap on your hands. We rescued Archie from overseas in November 2019. He is my first dog, and I don't know what I'd do without him.
